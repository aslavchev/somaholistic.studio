### 2. Security Improvement Needed

**The Problem:**
The booking form sends customer information to WhatsApp without cleaning it first.

**Why It Matters:**

**The Fix:**

### 3. Google Maps Not Connected

**The Problem:**
Your website doesn't link to your Google Maps listing where you already have great reviews.

**Why It Matters:**
- Clients can't easily get directions
- Your real Google rating isn't shown
- Missed opportunity to build trust with real reviews

**The Fix:**
Add "See on Google Maps" link and show your actual Google rating (instead of made-up numbers).

---

### 4. Google Can't Understand Your Business

**The Problem:**
Your website doesn't have special code that tells Google what your business is, where it's located, what services you offer, etc.

**Why It Matters:**
- Lower ranking in Google search results
- No rich information shown in search (like star ratings, address, hours)
- Harder for new clients to find you

**The Fix:**
Add structured data that Google uses to understand businesses.
